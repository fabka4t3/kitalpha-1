Preferences

Kitalpha's documentation generation provides some level of customization accessible from Eclipse Preferences menu.

Branding information are included in the generated HTML pages:

  • Copyright content in the footer of the pages
  • A Logo image in the header of the pages

When diagrams are included in the generation, their export format can be selected in the Diagrams preferences.

HTML pages extensions can be contributed through Eclipse extension points (see Extensibility page for further information). Contributed extensions can be (de)activated from the Page extensions preferences.
